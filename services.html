OUR STAFF

Angelo Valdez

Angelo Valdez, the owner and barber of TinTin the Barber, gained his experience while working at the famous Supremo barbershop. Once he had saved enough funds, he decided to open his own barbershop in Maysan, Valenzuela City. Known for his discipline and attention to detail, Angelo consistently delivers polished haircuts to his customers. His dedication has made TinTin the Barber a beloved spot among local patrons.

SERVICES

The barbershop provides several services such as haircut that can be just general haircuts or customized according to customer's preferences. They also provide shaving service where customers can get their beard or mustach trimmed or stylized. Lastly, the barbershop offers a home service where the barber personally visits their customers at their assigned location to provide them either of the services that were mentioned.

Haircut

Experience the precision and artistry of our professional haircutting services at TinTin the Barber. We are dedicated to delivering the perfect cut, tailored to your unique style and preferences. Whether you’re looking for a classic trim, a trendy new look, or a sophisticated fade, we use the finest techniques and tools to ensure every detail is flawless.

Shave

Indulge in the luxurious experience of our professional shaving services at TinTin the Barber. We offer a close and comfortable shave, using top-quality products to ensure your skin feels smooth and refreshed. Whether you prefer a clean shave or a precisely groomed beard, we cater to your specific needs with precision and care.

Home Service

Enjoy the convenience and comfort of our professional barbering services right in your own home with TinTin the Barber's home service. Our skilled barbers bring all the necessary tools and expertise to deliver high-quality haircuts, shaves, and grooming services at your doorstep.
