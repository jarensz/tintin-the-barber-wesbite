A DAY IN A LIFE IN A BARBERSHOP

Kuya Tin's day as a barber starts at 7 a.m. He wakes up to get ready for breakfast before heading to his shop. His open hours are 9:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. After having breakfast at home, he rides his bike to his shop. Sometimes, when he's running late, he takes his breakfast at the shop because there are many food stores nearby.

Once he opens the shop, he starts by preparing his tools and cleaning the shop before customers arrive. He sweeps the hair from the floor and cleans his haircut tools. After that, he turns on the air conditioning to refresh the inside of the store. Once all the preparations are done, he checks for messages from customers wanting to reserve a spot and notes them down so he knows who to prioritize when they arrive.
